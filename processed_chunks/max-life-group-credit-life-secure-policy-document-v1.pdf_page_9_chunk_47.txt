2.3. Termination of Insurance  
2.3.1.  The Insurance on  the life of a Member shall automatically cease on the earliest of the following dates:  
i. on the  Expiry D ate; or 
ii. on the date on which the death claim of the Member is admitted by Us; or  
iii. on the date of receipt of a valid request fo r surrender  of Insurance  from the Member  by Us .
