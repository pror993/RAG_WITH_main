Q.What is the procedure for recording any 
changes in the policy? 
Ans. If there are any changes in the policy , it will be 
done by an Endorsement by the insurance office. 
Submit a letter to the insurer with proof for the 
changes and obtain the Endorsement. Check the 
correctness of the Endorsement.
