9.7. Nomination  
9.7.1.  The person named by the Member as the Nominee in the enrollment form  for Insurance and whose 
name has been registered and recorded  by the Master Policyholder  in the Register o f Member s in 
accordance with Section 39 of the Insurance Act , shall be the person to whom the Death Benefit under 
this Policy will be paid by Us in the event of death of the Member.
